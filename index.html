Heres a timeline of Michael Jordans Life

  • 1963 -

    Born in Brooklyn, New York City

  • 1968 -

    Moved to Wilmington, North Carolina

  • 1982 -

    Played one year of college basketball with the North Carolina Tar Heels

  • 1984 -

    Joined the Bulls as the third overall draft pick

  • 1991 -

    Jordan won his first NBA title with the Bulls

  • 1992 & 1993 -

    Jordan won another 2 NBA titles securing a three-peat

  • 1994 -

    Jordan left the NBA for a year to play minor League Baseball with the Chicago White Sox organization

  • 1995 > 1999 -

    Jordan rejoined the bulls where he played for the next 4 years winning 3 more championships before retiring for a second time

  • 2001 > 2003 -

    Jordan returned for 2 more NBA seasons playing for the Washington Wizards before officially retiring

Michael Jordans career highlights

  • NBA Rookie of the Year (1985)

  • 6× NBA champion (1991–1993, 1996–1998)

  • 6× NBA Finals MVP (1991–1993, 1996–1998)

  • 10× All-NBA First Team (1987–1993, 1996–1998)
